EMERGENCY DENTAL

£55.00 to be seen and two small x-rays

At Wimbledon Smiles we try to provide SAME DAY emergency appointments where possible for only £55 for the examination and competitive rates for subsequent treatment. Our Dentists are experts in all emergency dental needs and can administer treatments such as composite fillings, extractions, and root canal work. After x-rays are taken our professionals will thoroughly assess the area of concern and diagnose the cause of pain. Your treatment options will be clearly explained to you and advice will be given in a compassionate way to put you at your ease.

The treatment uses a material very similar to that used for tooth-coloured fillings to disguise small imperfections and restore the appearance and functionality of your teeth. The tooth is prepared, usually with an etching solution, and we apply and shape the material, making it look natural. It is then set, or bonded, into place using a special light to blend with your natural teeth.

Your Local Dental Emergency Service

What should I expect during my appointment?

  • Provide relief from the immediate dental pain

  • Ensure the affected teeth and surrounding tissues are stabilised in order to prevent further damage.

  • To provide temporary pain relief, giving you the time to seek further care for definitive treatment options.
